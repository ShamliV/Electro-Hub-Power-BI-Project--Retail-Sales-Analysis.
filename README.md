📌 Project Overview
The Electro Hub Power BI project is an end‑to‑end retail analytics solution designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, product trends, and city‑level opportunities. The dashboard helps business users understand revenue drivers, identify growth opportunities, and make data‑driven decisions through interactive visualizations.

Business Objectives
Track overall sales, profit, and profit margin
Identify top and bottom performing products
Analyze sales trends across months and years
Understand city‑level sales contribution and opportunities
Evaluate category‑wise and product‑wise performance

Dashboard Highlights
1. Sales & Profit Overview
Total Sales: 122.31M
Total Profit: 12M
Profit Margin: ~10%
Strong alignment between sales and profitability indicating stable business performance

2. Product Performance Analysis
Top Revenue Drivers: Apple iPhone 14, Apple MacBook Air, Sony Bravia TV, Samsung Galaxy S21
Bottom Products: Tupperware Lunch Box, L’Oreal Shampoo, Nivea Body Lotion
Identification of star products and low‑revenue products for bundling or rationalization

3. City‑Level Analysis
City‑wise total sales and average sales per customer
Metro cities like Bangalore and Hyderabad show lower average sales per customer
Highlights strong upselling and promotional opportunities in metro regions


4. Sales Trends
Monthly sales trend analysis (2020–2024)
Identification of low‑sales months for targeted promotions
Seasonal trend visibility for planning campaigns


5. Opportunity Insights
Heavy reliance on electronics category
Mid‑performing products with high growth potential identified
Recommendations for cross‑selling and discount strategies

🛠 Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop
DAX for calculated measures and KPIs
Power Query for data cleaning and transformation

📈 Key Outcomes

Delivered actionable insights for management
Improved visibility into revenue drivers and underperforming areas
Supported business decisions on promotions, pricing, and product strategy
